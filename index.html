About Me

I am Glory Kampezeni, a fourth-year student of African Musicology at Malawi University of Science and Technology (MUST). My work explores the fusion of traditional Malawian music and modern technology. Through drumming, dance, and multimedia production, I aim to preserve our cultural identity while innovating for sustainable development.
